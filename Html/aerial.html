Aerial

An aerial is a technique of boosting your car into the air to hit the ball.

How to do?

To pull off an aerial, jump off the ground and then hit the boost
in mid-air, while you're pointed in the direction of the ball.
You'll need a steady thumb on the left stick to steer.
