Simple flick

Flicking is the act of flipping your car whilst dribbling to
give the ball power and height. This makes the shot difficult
to save and allows for easily getting the ball over an opponent
Here you will be taught the simple flick.

How to do?

To pull off a simple flick, first the ball has to be on the side
of which direction you flip to. Once the ball is correctly
positioned, simply jump into the air and flip into the direction
you want to flick to.
