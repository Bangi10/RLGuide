Fast aerial

A fast aerial is an aerial move which pushes you faster into the air than a regular aerial,
utilizing a boost to launch you off the ground at great speed.

How to do?

To do a fast aerial, you first need to jump, tilt back until your car is almost
completely vertical, then press jump again and boost upwards as you wish.
This process should be done very fast in order to execute the fast aerial properly.
