Air roll shots

In an Air roll shot, the player tilts the front corner of their car up
using air roll and dodges diagonally into the ball.

How to do?

To pull off an air roll shot, after or during your jump to approach
the ball, air roll your car so that the point of contact will pull
away from the center of the ball, then dodge diagonally so that the
point of contact for your shot is the front corner of your car.
