Redirects

When the ball is off-target, a player can redirect the ball
into the net as the pass is turned into a goal seamlessly.
You can redirect shots from the air, from a wall, from the backboard or even from the ceiling.
Some redirects are easy, and some are almost impossible.

How to do?

To redirect the ball, you need to position yourself in the air by aerialing,
tilt your car by air rolling in the perfect way so when the ball touches your car -
it will go straight into the net.
