Powershot

A powershot is when you hit the ball at the perfect angle, speed and direction
in order to hit the ball with an immense amount of power across the field.

How to do?

To pull off a powershot, jump off the ground and start "aerialing"
into the ball. Once you're close enough, flip forwards using your
second jump (will be available only for 1.5 seconds after first jump,
that's why you have do it quick) to kick the ball at a great velocity.
